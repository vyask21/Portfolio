Natural Disaster Management

This website was designed to spread information about all possible natural disasters occuring throughout the world and data of natural disasters occuring in real-time. This portal also holds information regarding the Do's and Don't's in order to safeguard oneself before a probable event, in the event of a disaster and after a disaster along with contact details to emergency services.

Career Guidance System

This is a web-based career guidance system that deals with an all-in-one portal for students seeking career guidance. The students can be divided on two basis, high school and senior year students. Students will be subjected to a test which will determine 3 sets of probable career paths for the students based on their qualitative, quantitave and verbal test scores.

Tableau Dashboard: Historic GHG Emissions Data

This dashboard depicts the emissions of greenhouse gase emissions across the world, based on the data collected from over a period of 28 years. This data has been used to visualize the emission contribution of different GHG gases throughout the years, country wise. This dashboard can be used to analyze and conclude based on the percentage and the type of gas emission contribution by each country in each year.

Power BI Dashboard: Real Time Analytics in Azure

This dashboard depicts the data from an Iot Hub simulation using message routing and event hubs. We've created the IoT hub and Event hub to receive and send messages from the simulated wind turbine.
